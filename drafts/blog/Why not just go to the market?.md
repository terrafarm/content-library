Selling can be time consuming. This system aims to reduce waste and risk by coordinating sales ahead of time. 

One worry is over-buying and waste on the consumption end. To reduce this problem, we make trading and donating possible. If a buyer gets bored or over stocked with a product, they can seek variety by trading individual vouchers. Charitable donations are also an option.

Some farms will have distribution pick-up days. Some will accept vouchers at a market booth. Some may deliver. And sometimes third-party delivery services will be available.
