Hugelkultur are no-dig raised beds. They hold moisture, build fertility, maximise surface volume and are great spaces for growing fruit, vegetables, and herbs. It's a relatively simple method for recycling heaps of woody debris and compost.

The article is introductory-level and has several photos.
