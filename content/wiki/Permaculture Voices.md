Diego Footer's podcast collection on farming and business. There are 4 running series:

1. [Grass fed life][1]: raising cows, pigs, and chickens profitably on pasture with farmer Darby Simpson.
2. [Farm small, farm smart, and the urban farmer][2]: focusing on small scale vegetable growers and how they run their operation profitably.
3. [Growing microgreens][3]: an inside look at how grower Chris Thoreau runs his profitable microgreen operation.
4. [Voices of change][4]: focusing on individuals who are trying to be the change that they want to see in the world.
