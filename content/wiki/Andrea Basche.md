Andrea Basche is a Kendall Science Fellow in the UCS Food & Environment program. Her research focuses on the potential for agroecological farming systems to reduce the risk and costs of increasing drought. In addition to her work on the benefits of ecologically-based farm practices, she helps further UCS research into the links between agriculture and climate.
